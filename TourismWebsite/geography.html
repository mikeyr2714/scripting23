Kamloops is situated within the Thompson River Delta, a basin fed from Shuswap Lake to the north, draining to Kamloops Lake to the south. Surrounded by mountains and hills Kamloops makes an ideal location for hiking and outdoor enthusiasts.

Kamloops is also nestled on the tip of the Sonoran Desert stretching from the far south of the United States to the interior of central B.C. With a hot climate in the summer, it offers the perfect living conditions of venomous bugs and reptiles.
