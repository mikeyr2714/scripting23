Kamloops’ economy has been made up of mostly mining, forestry, retail and agriculture. Following the introduction of Green Energy, Technology, Bioenergy, and a Tourism boom have contributed to the growth of Kamloops’ economy and the city.

Thompson Rivers University has contributed to the research and development of practical uses for Renewable Energy within the province of British Columbia. Kamloops also has the most golf courses per capita in Canada. Leading for many of the population to pick up the sport, as well as bring many golf tournaments to the city every summer.
