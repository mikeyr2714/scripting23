Kamloops is in the heart of the Okanagan Valley, British Columbia. Founded in 1811 and fully recognized as a city in 1893.

Kamloops grew to a population of just over 90,000 in 2016. Currently 130 years old Kamloops is an old city with a new style all throughout the city. The ideal place to start a family or grow a business, the city has a thriving economy and amazing city life.
